The Castle of San Basilio

How to reach it and where to stay


Maria& Ivan

The ceremony will start at 18.00


The majestic Castle of San Basilio is located a few kilometers from the city center of Marconia, and precisely on the provincial road that connects Marconia to Marina di Pisticci, in the direction of the famous 106 Jonica National Road that connects Calabria to Basilicata and Puglia.

For relatives and friends arriving from Ginosa, the most convenient route is through the SS580 to the intersection for Reggio Calabria and proceed on the SS106 for a few km to the Marconia exit (Pisticci / Marconia / San Basilio). From there you will find directions and in a few minutes you will have arrived. Total journey time about 30 minutes.
Alternatively you can take the provincial road 2 which crosses the SPexSS175, which takes you straight on the SS106.

For those coming from far, we recommend taking a flight to Bari. From there there are several options. For those who want to be independent and spend a few days around Puglia, can rent a car. Alternatively there are several buses or trains from Bari to Ginosa, Castellaneta. Matera. In any case, we and our family would be willing to welcome you at the airport or organize a shuttle that will take you straight to the Castle.
