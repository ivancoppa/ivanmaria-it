The Castle of San Basilio

A magic place

At the point where the sea and the land meet, between the blue of the Ionian coast and the green shades of the metapontine hills, stands the Castle of San Basilio, a magical and refined place.
We would be delighted to be able to celebrate our wedding with our dearest people in this enchanting and suggestive place.

The Castle has an ancient history that dates back to the 9th century when the Basilian monks were the first to build a monastery in this strip of Lucanian land, then the monastery became a Norman castle and therefore fortified with its watchtower (12th century) . After the Normans there were other ecclesiastical settlements with the monastic communities of the Benedictines and the Carthusians. In 1830 the Estate of San Basilio was bought by the Ferrante family of Ruffano and subsequently sold to the Marquis Berlingieri, who are its current owners.

Tavole Palatine

Metaponto

Le Tavole Palatine sono i resti di un tempio dorico periptero esastilo del VI secolo a.C. dedicato alla divinità mitologica Hera.

Gravina di Ginosa

Ginosa (TA)

La gravina di Ginosa è una incisione erosiva profonda, che fa parte dell'area delle Gravine, un'area naturale protetta sul percorso di un antico corso d'acqua.

Grotte di Castellana

Le Grotte di Castellana sono un complesso di cavità sotterranee di origine carsica di interesse speleologico e turistico.
